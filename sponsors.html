VENUE SPONSOR

Dickens Opera House

Our home base for Revenge at the Opera House and the place Longmont gathers for a night of pro wrestling.

Historic facts

  • Built in 1881 and operating by 1882 as Longmont's community venue.
  • Named for builder William Henry Dickens, a relative of Charles Dickens.
  • Mixed-use from day one: bank and storefronts on the first floor, auditorium upstairs.
  • Survived the 1905 Masonic Temple fire that threatened the block.

Sources: National Park Service, Dickens Opera House, City of Longmont.
